Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring a song for us to learn?

Absolutely, I encourage it. Not only is it good to play something you like listening to, it gives us a framework to put some new skills and ideas into action.

Can I pay for a block of lessons?

For sure. You can purchase lessons in blocks of 5 or more. It's a great way to keep focused and committed.

I'm not sure if I want to commit to purchasing a block of lessons, do you offer one-off sessions?

I sure do! I understand that you might want to find out if my teaching style suits you, so a one-off lesson would be perfect. For these lessons, I charge $5 on top of the block lesson rate.

Do I have to come to you or can you travel to my house?

Either. You can have lessons at my home studio, or for an extra charge I can come to you.
